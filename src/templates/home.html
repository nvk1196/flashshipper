FAQ

Frequently Ask Questions

What do we do?

Flash Shipper is a complete shipping service. We will pick up, package, and ship your item with just a few clicks.

Pick up time is around 20 minutes. All package come with USPS tracking number and insured.

How can I ship many items as once?

We are currently not picking up and packaging many item at once. However, we could treat these items as 1 large item in term of size and weight.

If you are asked to select the size for your item, you will need to select the size of these items as they are bundle together. Total weight of these items can not exceed 70 lbs.

My store didn't give me a shipping label.

You could choose to ship your item in a regular way.

Select "No" when being asked "Is this a return item from an online merchandise store with a prepaid label?". Enter the ship to address and ship your item like a regular item.

How much does it cost?

Pick up and regular package fee is 5$.

Fragile item packaging is 2$ extra.

Shipping cost depend on your item's size range from 8-20$.
